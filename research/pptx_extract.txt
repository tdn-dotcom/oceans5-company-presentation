WHO WE ARE
An independent specialist firm for marineengineering and operations
Oceans5 is a Norwegian specialist firm founded in 2021. We combine engineering services, project management and operational execution in one organisation — so clients meet the same team from concept to completed installation.
We work primarily in marine installations, power infrastructure and aquaculture — with a focus on offshore wind and cable work. Three companies in the group (Oceans5 Group AS, Oceans5 AS and O5 Operations AS) let us deliver both engineering services and operational crew.
